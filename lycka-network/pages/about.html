About Lycka Network

Kenya's trusted domestic staffing agency — built on integrity, training, and care.

Who We Are

Connecting Homes With Trusted Workers

Lycka Network Househelps Bureau was founded to solve one of Nairobi's most persistent challenges — finding reliable, vetted, and trained domestic help quickly and safely.

We operate as a professional domestic staffing agency, handling everything from recruitment and background checks to training, placement, and ongoing support.

Our name, Lycka (Swedish for "happiness"), reflects our belief that a well-staffed home creates happiness for everyone — employers and workers alike.

300+ Workers in our database
150+ Target placements in Year 1
100% Background checked workers
14+ Towns served across Kenya

🎯 Our Mission

To provide reliable, well-trained, and vetted domestic workers while empowering individuals with stable employment opportunities and dignified working conditions.

🌟 Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading and most trusted domestic staffing agency — known for quality, speed, integrity, and the wellbeing of both clients and workers.
